# Video Marketing & YouTube Optimization Project  

## Project Type
Academic / Practice Project

## Project Overview
This project focuses on planning a video marketing strategy and optimizing YouTube content to increase visibility, engagement, and reach.

## Objectives
- Improve video discoverability on YouTube  
- Increase engagement through optimized content  
- Understand YouTube SEO fundamentals  

## Work Done
- Keyword research for video titles and descriptions  
- Optimized video titles, tags, and descriptions  
- Planned content and upload schedule  
- Designed thumbnail strategy  
- Identified key YouTube performance metrics  

## Tools Used
- YouTube Studio  
- Canva  
- Keyword research tools (basic)  

## Metrics Tracked
- Views  
- Watch Time  
- Click-Through Rate (CTR)  
- Subscriber Growth  

## Outcome
- Developed a structured video marketing plan  
- Improved understanding of YouTube optimization techniques
